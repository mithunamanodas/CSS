About Cool Luxury Resort

Amidst an ambiance of complete serenity and picture-perfect surrounding, Ramada is a luxury retreat in a massive sprawling area of 8 acres. A short drive from Cochin, yet far away from the hustle of the capital city, it is a pure haven for the urban dwellers. Termed as the top-line of the best resorts in Kochi, it offers the best of both worlds – extreme luxury and calming rejuvenation. Ramada Resort is the most popular choice among travelers for a relaxing, luxurious stay. What’s special: 105 m long lazy-river swimming pool, Kerala’s biggest spa, sunken pool bar, and floating pool villas Suggested suite: Presidential Suite Room ensures a luxurious stay. Also, you can indulge a little and enjoy the perks of floating pool villas. Tariff: INR 12,000 per night, onwards.
